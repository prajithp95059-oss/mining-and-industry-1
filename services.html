Open-Pit Operations

Surface Mining

Our surface mining division operates some of the world's largest open-pit and strip-mining complexes. We deploy the latest Komatsu and Caterpillar autonomous fleets, precision GPS-guided drill rigs, and AI-optimised blast designs to maximise ore recovery while minimising dilution and environmental disturbance.

  • Open-pit mine design and pushback sequencing
  • Precision blasting with AI-optimised fragmentation models
  • Autonomous and semi-autonomous haulage systems
  • Slope stability monitoring with real-time InSAR radar
  • Waste rock management and tailings storage facilities
  • Continuous benchmarking against global productivity standards

Shaft & Tunnel Operations

Underground Mining

STACKLY's underground division operates deep shaft and tunnel mines from 200m to 3,200m depth. Our technically sophisticated teams deploy sub-level caving, block caving, and cut-and-fill stoping methods, supported by state-of-the-art ventilation, ground support, and refuge systems.

  • Shaft sinking and tunnel boring machine operations
  • Sub-level caving and block cave management
  • Automated battery-electric underground fleets
  • Real-time seismic monitoring and ground control
  • Underground ore sorting and pre-concentration
  • Refuge chamber installation and emergency response systems

Comminution to Smelting

Mineral Processing

Our processing plants transform run-of-mine ore into high-grade mineral concentrates, applying the full suite of comminution, concentration, hydrometallurgical, and pyrometallurgical processes. We operate 14 processing plants globally with a combined annual throughput of 280 million tonnes.

  • Primary, secondary, and tertiary crushing circuits
  • SAG and ball mill grinding with AI-controlled load optimisation
  • Froth flotation for sulphide and oxide ore streams
  • Carbon-in-leach and carbon-in-pulp gold recovery circuits
  • Heap leach and SX-EW copper extraction
  • Tailings management and water recycling systems

Exploration & Modelling

Geological Surveys

Our geological services division provides world-class exploration, resource estimation, and mine geology services. We integrate airborne geophysics, LiDAR, drone surveying, and AI-driven 3D modelling to accurately characterise and quantify mineral resources from early-stage exploration through to bankable feasibility study.

  • Airborne magnetic, gravity, and electromagnetic surveys
  • RC and diamond core drilling programmes
  • JORC/NI 43-101 resource estimation and classification
  • 3D ore body modelling with implicit surface techniques
  • Geometallurgical characterisation and hardness mapping
  • Geostatistical analysis and grade simulation
