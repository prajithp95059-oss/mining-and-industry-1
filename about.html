Our Purpose

We Don't Just Extract Resources — We Build Legacies

Founded in 1998 by a team of pioneering geologists and mining engineers, STACKLY started with a single open-pit iron ore operation in Western Australia. Today, we are a globally recognised mining and industrial services company operating on six continents.

Our philosophy is simple: extract responsibly, operate safely, and leave communities and ecosystems better than we found them. This ethos has driven every decision we have made over the past 25 years — from the way we design our mine plans to the way we hire our people.

We believe that mining is not just an industry — it is a cornerstone of modern civilisation, providing the raw materials that power electric vehicles, renewable energy infrastructure, and advanced technology. Our mission is to do this work with uncompromising integrity.

Our Journey

25 Years of Milestones

1998

Foundation — Western Australia

STACKLY Mining is founded in Perth by Dr. Alan Stackworth and a team of 12 geologists. First iron ore contract awarded in the Pilbara region.

2003

First International Expansion

Secured first overseas contract in South Africa's Witwatersrand gold belt. Established the STACKLY Safety Charter — zero-harm pledge that still guides us today.

2009

Listed on ASX

Successful ASX listing raised $400M, funding major expansion into Chile copper operations and state-of-the-art processing facilities.

2015

Automation Pioneer

Deployed the world's largest autonomous haul truck fleet — 240 vehicles — at our Pilbara operations. Awarded Innovation Mine of the Year.

2020

Net-Zero Commitment

Announced carbon neutrality target for 2035. Commissioned first solar-powered processing plant in the Atacama. Ranked #1 in ESG performance among ASX-listed mining companies.

2024

AI-Powered Mining Hub

Opened the STACKLY Digital Mining Hub in Sydney — a global operations control centre managing 28 active mines in real time using AI and predictive analytics.
