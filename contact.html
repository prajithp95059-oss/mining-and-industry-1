Common Questions

FAQ CONTACT

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

+

All general inquiries receive an initial response within 24 business hours. For urgent technical or operational matters, our emergency line is available around the clock with a guaranteed 1-hour response time.

Do you sign NDAs before project discussions?

+

Absolutely. We routinely sign mutual NDAs before discussing any project details. Our legal team can prepare a standard NDA within 24 hours, or we're happy to review your preferred template.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

+

Helpful details include the project location, commodity type, estimated scale of operations, project timeline, and budget range. Don't worry if you don't have all the details — we'll work through the requirements together during the discovery call.

Can you work on projects outside your listed countries?

+

Yes. While we have established offices in India, Australia, South Africa, and the UK, our project teams operate globally. We have completed projects in over 22 countries and can mobilise rapidly to new locations.

Do you offer feasibility studies for new projects?

+

Yes. Feasibility studies are a core part of our pre-project services. We conduct scoping, pre-feasibility, and bankable feasibility studies aligned with JORC, NI 43-101, and SAMREC standards.

What is the typical timeline for a project proposal?

+

Following an initial discovery call, we typically deliver a detailed technical and commercial proposal within 5–7 business days for standard engagements. Complex multi-phase proposals may take up to 15 business days.
